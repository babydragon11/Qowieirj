Welcome to Tollgate Farms 4-H Club

Empowering youth through hands-on learning, leadership, and agriculture right here in our community.

About Our Club

Based at the historic Tollgate Farms, our 4-H club focuses on building life skills, fostering leadership, and exploring agricultural interests. From livestock and horticulture to crafting and civic duty, our youth members "learn by doing."

📢 Fair Season is Around the Corner! Remember to keep your record books up to date. Our project sign-ups close before the next regular meeting.

Meeting Details
